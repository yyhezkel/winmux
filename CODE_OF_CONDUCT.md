# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be kind, be respectful, assume good faith. Behavior that targets people for who they are — rather than what they say or build — is not welcome here.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, email **yyhezkel@gmail.com**. Reports will be handled confidentially.

The full Contributor Covenant text is available at the link above and applies in full to this project.
