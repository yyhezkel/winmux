PROGRESS LOG — append-only. Newest entries at bottom. Rotate as PROGRESS_OLD_<date>.txt when this file gets unwieldy.

Entry format:

==================================================================
[YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM TZ] session=<id-or-tag>
TASK: <one-line summary>
FILES:
  M <path>
  A <path>
  D <path>
RESULT: <verified-live | compiles-untested | reverted | partial>
NOTES: <optional — anything a future session would want to grep for>
==================================================================
